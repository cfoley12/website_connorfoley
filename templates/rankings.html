What are Speed Ratings?

We were inspired by a New York-based website (tullyrunners.com) that allows high school runners in New York to compare thier times with competitors across different courses. Using race results from athletic.net, we have tried to condct a similar analysis of ten courses in Michigan. All of our selected courses held more than 5 indiviudal races in the Fall 2015 cross country season.
